# Privacy Policy

Depth Map Extractor is a simple macOS utility that extracts a 16-bit depth map from a photo.

## Summary

- No account required.
- No analytics or tracking.
- No data collection or sale.

## Data processing

All processing happens locally on your Mac. Photos you open or drag into the app are used only to perform the conversion and are not transmitted off-device.

## Data storage

Depth Map Extractor does not store or retain your photos or any extracted depth maps. The only files created are the output files you choose to export/save.

## Network access

Depth Map Extractor does not send your data to any server and does not include third-party SDKs for advertising or analytics.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue in this repository.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. The latest version will be available in this file.
